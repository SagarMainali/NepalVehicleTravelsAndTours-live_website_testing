Who are we?

History

We Nepal Vehilce Travels And Tours started our journey in 2002 A.D. Since then we have been scaling as a company to do what we do best. Our mission and quality of service to out clients has always been the same from day one. All these years we have gained so much experience in what we do, which is also our main strength. These years of experience has helped us to acknowledge the quality of service that client needs. As of now, we are in a position where we can proudly say that we are one of the best vehicle rental service providing company in Nepal.

Services

Our main service is to make our clients happy and make sure that they meet their expectations from us. We do this by providing vehicles, driving to their destination and bringing them home back safely. For this we recruit only professional drivers that are comfortable to drive on the roads of Nepal. The quality of service we provide have been loved by our clients all these years and have only received positive feedbacks. However, we are open to receive any kind of feedbacks from our clients.

Mission & Vision

Since the establishment of our company, our main focus has been providing the best experience to the clients. This has never changed since 2002. We are determined to upgrade our quality of service day by day. We make sure the quality of vehicles we use gets upgraded time to time and we only hire professinals. We believe that clients should get most out of their investment from us. Our mission is to be the best vehicle rental service providing company in nepal in the upcoming years.
