Nepal Vehicle Tours And Travels provides different category of vehicles. We provide vehicle service for every type of trips. Our vehicle travels all over Nepal for any number of days. We have our own drivers who are highly experienced to drive on the roads of Himalayan, Hilly and Terai region of Nepal. The vehicles are well-maintained and clean considering comfortness of our customers.

Our Vehicle Categories

Car

Among all the other vehicles we offer, car is the most affordable and flexible. Cars are suitable for around 1-4 people. We offer different sedan cars of different brands. The best places to travel with cars are around the valley which covers Kathmandu, Bhaktapur and Lalitpur. Our car travels to far destinations also but it may not be a good option considering comfortness of the travellers. Since the roads outside the valley are mostly offroads, we suggest the travellers to go for jeep. However, we also respect our customer's choice and if you want to go for quick sideseeing around the valley or want airport pickup and dropoff service form us, we highly recommend this category.

Jeep

Jeeps are a good choice if travellers want to go outside the valley areas. The comfortness it provides along with the space for few luggages and few more people, jeeps are the best alternative for cars. The Jeeps are suitable for around 5-8 people. The jeeps goes well with the condition of roads outslide the valley. Since travellers also have some luggage when they visit places far from the valley, jeeps are suitalbe in this case as well. We provide variety of 2WD and 4WD jeeps in affordable price that can travel anywhere with ease.

Van

Vans have larger room for people than cars and jeeps due to its spacious nature. People usually like to travel in a van if they are bigger in number but does not want to travel much far away from the valley. Vans are around 7-11 seater at max. The short and medium distance travel is best suited with this type of vehicle. This is not a flexible vehicle for further distance in hilly and mountain regions of Nepal because of its low suspension height which may cause problems in offroads. However, this could be the best choice even while having long trips when travelling to Terai region of Nepal since the road condition are good.

Hiace

Hiace vehicle are the alternative to the regular vans. The design of the regular van and the hiace vans are almost identical. The regular vans are of at least 3 types unlike hiace. The hiace offers more space for luggage and people. It has the seat for around 14 people. It is a good choice for family trips outside or inside the valley. The seats are very comfortable in this type of vehilce providing enough space for legs as well. Hiace vans are larger in size than the regular vans and jeep as well. They are specifically not designed to handle offroads therefore Terai region tour would be best with this vehicle.

Coaster

Coaster are mini-bus having the seat capacity of around 20-24 people. Along with this, there is extra space for your luggage as well. The seating position is much more higher giving the travellers the experience of better view on the trip. Due to the large size of the vehicle, travellers feel comfortable through their tour. Coaster are preferred during far trips as they can handle certain type of offroads although they are not specifically designed for offroads. This vehilce could be a good choice for the families going to picnic and ceremonies like Janti as well.

Bus

Among all types of vehicle we provide to our travellers, bus is the largest category. The seat capacity of this type of vehilce is around 30-40. You can already assume the space it provides for your luggages due to its gigantic nature. This vehilce can easily handle offroads and long trips without travellers feeling discomfort. The seating position is way higher than any other type of vehilce giving you the best view experience along your whole trip. This is usually a good choice when the travellers are huge in number and the destination is much far. Our travellers for this type of vehicle are usually those going for picnic, long-stay, Janti and Tirtha Yatras.
